ABOUT CHS

Read about CHS and the origins of our company

Creative Home Sales was the brainchild of Martha Ehlert and Joyce Austin as seen above. Martha is the daughter of Joyce and together they work as a team. The work they do is directly attributed to housing and real estate, Martha used to do a lot of work in HUD Housing before working at an apartment complex. Joyce worked in HUD Housing for years before getting her RE License, allowing her the freedom to sell homes that weren't only attached to HUD. Joyce and Martha recently decided to work together on a project that they asked Martha's son Malachi to build a website for; Creative Home Sales. Malachi and Meakoda are the two founding members of Orion Solutions, a Freelancing organization that privides IT Consulting, Web Development and other freelance jobs. Together, these four are building an empire known as Creative Home Sales, attributing it with Orion Solutions.
